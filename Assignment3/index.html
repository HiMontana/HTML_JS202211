Bio

When I was younger I loved playing on my DS. I played Super Smash Brothers and Mario Kart all day. I even brought it to school one day and got it taken away. It was an unfortunate experience. It wasn’t until l 2011 I played the first game I was absolutely addicted to this game Call of Duty Black Ops.

One day they found some glitch in the game that allowed them to play a 4 player split screen. At first, they were playing with two of their friends but then one went home and they asked me to play. It was my first time playing with a controller. I was so bad then I gradually got better and better. I didn’t get my own console until years later that’s when I really got into video games. I would stay up all day and night playing Grand Theft Auto Online with my friends and my mom would get mad at me. I am a certified console gamer and now I am an anime nerd so… yeah that’s me.
